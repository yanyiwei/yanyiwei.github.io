Hi! I'm Yiwei Yang.


I am a 4th year undergraduate student at the University of Michigan, and am currently doing research with Professor Walter Lasecki in the CRO+MA(Crowds+Machines) lab. I am interested in the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Specifically, I want to create Hybrid Intelligence systems that leverage both human and machine intelligence to tackle problems that cannot be solved by either alone.
