Hi, I am Yiwei Yang!

I am a fourth year undergraduate studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan. I am advised by Professor Walter Lasecki and am doing research in the CRO+MA(Crowds+Machines) Lab.

Research

My research interest lies broadly at the intersection of HCI and AI. Currently, my research focuses on leveraging human expert knowledge to generalize ML models over unseen data.
